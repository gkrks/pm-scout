Product Manager, Payments

About the Role

We are looking for a Product Manager to join our Payments team and help build the next generation of payment infrastructure.

What You'll Do

  • Define product vision and roadmap for our payments platform
  • Partner with engineering, design, and data teams to ship features
  • Conduct user research to identify pain points and opportunities
  • Define and monitor KPIs and success metrics for product launches

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of product management experience in a B2B or fintech environment
  • Experience with payment systems such as ACH, card networks, or wire transfers
  • Proven ability to work cross-functionally with engineering and design teams
  • Strong SQL skills for data analysis and metrics tracking
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent experience
  • Experience conducting A/B testing and running experiments
  • Familiarity with APIs and developer-facing products
  • Track record of shipping 0-to-1 products at scale

Nice to Have

  • Experience with Stripe, Adyen, or similar payment processors
  • MBA or graduate degree
  • Knowledge of PCI compliance and financial regulations

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and equity
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • Unlimited PTO
